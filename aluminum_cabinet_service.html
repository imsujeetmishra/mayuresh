Aluminum Cabinet Service

Our Aluminum Cabinets are best in quality and supplied in the market with wheels for easy shifting. These cabinets are reliable in working and crack proof in nature. Offered cabinets are hard framed and available in the market with multiple racks and sizes. In addition to this, these aluminum cabinets are praised for pressure tolerance.

We provide crca, ss, aluminum from 8mm thickness to 3mm thickness. Our expert staff takes care to convert the raw material into one of the finest metal cabinet. It caters to the need of the clients. With superior finish and durability at the competitive price, it is finding the attention of everyone. The attractive finish and long life are other key attraction. They can be also painted or powder coated as per client requirement.
